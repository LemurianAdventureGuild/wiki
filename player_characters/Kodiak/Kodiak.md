# Kodiak (i.e. Cody)

![Cody](kodiak.png)
Shadar-Kai, Druid

## Backstory

Cody(Kodiak) was a devout druid to the Circle of the Morning Draft.  The Circle sent him to broker peace between the town of Oakhaven and the Gloomfang Goblins.  During a nightly conflict, Mayor Karina of Oakhaven rushed to Cody’s tent, needing his help. She threw open the flap and found Cody happily sleeping in his "birthday suit," achieving a deep, powerful, and very exposed communion with a bed of moss.

Horrified and enraged by this "Scourge of Decency," Mayor Karina denounced him publicly. The peace talks collapsed instantly. Both the town and the goblins, insulted by his indecency, blamed Cody for renewed hostilities.  Cody was summarily excommunicated from his own Circle, not for the nudity, but for the unnatural chaos it created. He is now a wandering, disgraced druid.  Cody currently calls the Lemurian Guild his new home, secretly hoping to do some sort of penance to get back into his Druidic Circle.
